What a convolution is. Slide a small weight grid (the kernel) across the image. At every position, multiply overlapping pixels by the kernel weights and sum. Sharp weights (big positive, big negative) give the output a big magnitude — that's how a CNN "lights up" when the kernel's pattern matches the image patch underneath.
