The Big Idea
Attention = "who should I listen to?"
When you read the word "it" in "The cat sat on the mat because it was tired", your brain knows "it" refers to the cat. Self-attention is how transformers do this: for each word, it computes how much to pay attention to every other word.

Sentence

Temperature (Softmax)

τ (sharpness) 1.00
Lower τ = sharper attention (focus on one word). Higher τ = more even (distributed).

The 3 Matrices

Every word gets turned into three vectors:

● Query (Q) — "What am I looking for?"
● Key (K) — "What do I contain?"
● Value (V) — "What do I deliver?"

Attention from word A to word B = softmax(Q_A · K_B) — how much A's query matches B's key.

How It Scales Up

Real transformers have many attention heads (8-96) that learn different relationships. GPT-4 uses multi-head attention in every one of its ~100+ layers. Each head might track grammar, semantics, or long-range references. All powered by the exact operation you see below.
