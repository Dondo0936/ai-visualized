How It Works

A perceptron is the simplest neural unit. It computes a weighted sum of inputs plus a bias, then applies a step function:

output = step(w1*x + w2*y + b)

The decision boundary is the line where w1*x + w2*y + b = 0. Points on one side are class 0, the other side class 1.

Formula

y = step(w · x + b)
