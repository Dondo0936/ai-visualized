The Big Idea
"Tell me who your neighbors are..."
K-Nearest Neighbors is the simplest classifier imaginable: to classify a new point, find the K closest known points and vote. Yet this basic idea powers massive systems: semantic search, recommendation engines, RAG (retrieval-augmented generation) all use nearest-neighbor search over embedding vectors.

Scaling Up

Real vector databases (Pinecone, Weaviate, pgvector) search billions of embeddings using approximate KNN (HNSW, IVF). They're behind: semantic search, image search, RAG in LLM apps, recommendation systems. Same algorithm, just smarter indexing.
