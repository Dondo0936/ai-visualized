What an activation does. Linear layers by themselves can only represent straight-line relationships. Activations are the only source of non-linearity in a network — they bend the signal so stacks of linear layers can approximate curved, complex functions. Pick the right one and training is smooth; pick the wrong one and gradients vanish or neurons go dead.
